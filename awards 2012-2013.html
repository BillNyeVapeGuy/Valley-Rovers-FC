Valley Rovers F.C. Player Awards 2012- 2013

Valley Rovers FC end of season awards ceremony was held in Ballyfarnon on 27th June 2013

Photos by Brian Farrell

There were 5 catagories of winners for this season


1. Player of the year
2. Most improved player over the year
3. Most dedicated player at training

 
Two additional awards were reveled, which were:
4. First ever goal scored for Valley Rovers FC by a girl.
5. First goal scored by our under 12's girls team.
